About us

Duckpond is a creative studio where code meets design. The name reflects a quiet source of inspiration — a place where ideas surface gently, like ripples on water.
Blending programming, web design, and illustration, we bring ideas to life through thoughtful, handcrafted digital experiences.
Our goal is to create work that feels alive: designs that resonate, code that flows, and illustrations that tell a story.
